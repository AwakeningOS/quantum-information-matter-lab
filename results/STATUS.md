## Status labels

```text

  generated by committed script and committed raw log; reproducibility check passes or equivalent check is documented

  generated by committed script and committed observation log; records trajectories without PASS/FAIL promotion

  conceptual or exploratory note; not a result claim

  old, failed, or unsupported claim retained for history only

META:
  repository management or migration file
```
